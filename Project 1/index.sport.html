My Favorite Sports

My favorite sport is cricket. Cricket is a very popular sport in countries like Pakistan, India, and England. I grew up in Pakistan, where cricket is very famous, and the children play this game in their homes as well as on the streets. It is a game people of all ages can watch and play. It was played between two teams, and each team has 11 players. This game became my favorite since I was a kid, and as I grew up, I played it with my brother.
