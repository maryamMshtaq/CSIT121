Mint Margarita

Refresh Your body With this Delicious Stawberry Juice this Summer

Ingredients

  • 2 cups strawberries â€“ cleaned
  • Sugar 2 teaspoons
  • 1/4 tsp Black Salt
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 Cup 7up Lime or Sprite (250ml)
  • Ice cubes as required

Prepration

  1. Add stawberries into the blender along with lemon juice, water black salt, sugar, and ice cubes. Blend it a couple times to completely smash the ice.
  2. Invert the glass rim with caster sugar. Then fill each glass with half a 7-Up or Sprite and pour the strawberry mixture over until the glass is full. Add a slice of strawberry to the glass rim as a garnish. Serve right away.
