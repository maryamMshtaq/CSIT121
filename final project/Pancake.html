Banana Pancakes

Start your morning with this fluffy, moist, soft, and flovourful banana pancakes.

Ingredients

  • Banana 1
  • Milk 1 cup
  • Egg 1 large
  • All purpose flour 2 tablespoon
  • Sugar 1 tabel spoon
  • Salt pinch
  • Baking powder pinch
  • vanilla extract
  • Honey for drizzling
  • Butter as required
  • Vegetable oil 1tsp

Prepration

  1. Take a bowl. Add the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt to a bowl, combine well, and then keep it aside.
  2. Then take another bowl and mash the banana until it is smooth with the help of a fork, then add the egg and mix well.
  3. Mix the milk and vanilla extract into the banana mixture.
  4. Pour the banana mixture along with 1 tablespoon of melted butter into the dry ingredients.
  5. Fold the batter with a rubber spatula. It will be thick and lumpy.
  6. Take a nonstick pan. Add vegetable oil and some butter, and heat it. After that, spoon the pancakes onto the griddle and cook until some bubbles appear on the top and the underside is golden brown and crisp.
  7. Place the ready pancakes on a plate and top them with some banana slices and a drizzle of honey. Serve it hot.
