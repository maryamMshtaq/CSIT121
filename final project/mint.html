Mint Margarita

A Summer Refreshing Drink

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • Mint leaves 4-5
  • 3 Lemons (Juice
  • 1 Cup 7up Lime or Sprite (250ml)
  • 3-4 Cups Ice cubes

Prepration

  1. Blend the ice cubes, water, mint leaves, salt, sugar, and lemon juice in a blender. Blend it a couple times to completely smash the ice.
  2. Fill each glass with half of 7up/sprite and pour the mint mixture over till the glass is full. Add some mint leaves or a slice of lemon to the glass rim as a garnish. Serve right away.
