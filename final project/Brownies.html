Brownies

Symbol of joy, happiness, and celebration, "The Brownies".

Ingredients

  • Dark chocolate 150 grams
  • Vegetable oil 40 ml
  • Butter 50 grams
  • Eggs 2 large (room temperature)
  • Refined sugar 150 grams
  • Baking powder pinch
  • vanilla extract
  • All-purpose flour 100 grams
  • Cocoa powder 35 grams

Prepration

  1. Preheat the oven to 150 °C.
  2. Melt the butter, oil, and dark chocolate chips in a bowl over a double boiler.
  3. Remove the double boiler from the chocolate mixture and let it cool at room temperature.
  4. Mix the eggs and sugar in a bowl. Blend thoroughly by whisking. Do not create any froth.
  5. Remix after adding the chocolate mixture.
  6. Lastly, add all purpose flour and mix until well combined.
  7. Line a 20 x 20 cm square baking tin with a parchment paper and pour the mixture in it. Bake at 150 °C for 20 minutes .
  8. Allow to cool and frost with Nutella.
