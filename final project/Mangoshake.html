Mango Shake

Give healthy addition to your life with Mango Shake

Ingredients

  • Mango 1
  • Milk 300 ml
  • Cucumber 1
  • sugar 1 tablespoon
  • Ice cubes- as needed
  • Copped Almonds 3-4

Prepration

  1. Take a mango, peel it, and cut it into smaller cubes.
  2. Then take a blender and add mango, milk, sugar, and ice cubes.
  3. Grind it for about 2 minutes.
  4. Serve it in a glass, garnish with chopped almonds, and enjoy a refreshing mango shake.

Mango Shake have many nutrition properties. It is helpful for ones who want to gain weight. Beside this if a person drinks mango shake daily it prevent you from skin breakout, boost your immunity and promote collage production in your skin.
